We often disregard the fact that 92% of the world’s data was created in the last 3-4 years. The future is nothing less of a data catastrophe. In the future if no solution is developed we might see data blackmarkets, data rationing. Deep learning can help us fight this problem by building intelligent compressors and cryptographers. We use a solution of this niche problem to solve every low speed internet’s consumer’s nightmare. We are no stranger to the situation where we just love a movie but we don’t have access to it’s HD print. Or when we simply switch to low resolution to save our data over the net. We plan to integrate these two concepts together.
